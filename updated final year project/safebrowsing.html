Safe Browsing

Why Safe Browsing Matters

Browsing the internet without precautions can expose you to malicious websites, phishing attempts, and unwanted software. Safe browsing ensures your data and devices stay protected.

Safe Browsing Tips

  • Always check for HTTPS and a lock icon before entering personal information.
  • Avoid downloading files from unverified websites.
  • Use an ad blocker and pop-up blocker for extra safety.
  • Keep your browser updated and avoid suspicious links.
