Social Engineering

Understanding Social Engineering

Social engineering is the manipulation of people into giving up confidential information. It exploits human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities.

Interactive Scenario (Mock-up)

A person calls claiming to be from IT support, urgently asking for your login credentials to fix a system issue.

What do you do?

  • A. Give them your credentials to help them fix it faster
  • B. Refuse and report the call to your IT department
  • C. Ask them to call back later when your supervisor is available

How to Protect Yourself

  • Always verify the identity of unknown callers or emailers.
  • Never share passwords or sensitive data over the phone or email.
  • Be cautious of urgent or emotional appeals.
  • Report suspicious interactions to security teams immediately.
