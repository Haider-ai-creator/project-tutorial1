Password Security

Why Password Security Matters

Weak or reused passwords are one of the most common causes of account breaches. Cybercriminals use password-guessing tools and leaked data to gain access to your online accounts.

Interactive Scenario (Mock-up)

You use the same password — "Password123" — for your email, banking, and social media accounts. One of these services gets hacked.

What could happen?

  • A. Nothing, since your accounts are unrelated
  • B. Only the hacked account is affected
  • C. All your accounts are at risk due to password reuse

Best Practices

  • Use a unique password for each account.
  • Include uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Use a password manager to keep track of your passwords securely.
  • Avoid personal information in passwords (e.g., names or birthdates).
