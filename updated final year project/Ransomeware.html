Ransomware

What is Ransomware?

Ransomware is a type of malicious software that encrypts your files or locks your system until a ransom is paid. It often spreads through email attachments, compromised websites, or software vulnerabilities.

Interactive Scenario (Mock-up)

You open an attachment from an unknown email sender. Moments later, all your files are encrypted, and a ransom message appears demanding payment in Bitcoin.

  • B. Avoided opening suspicious attachments and kept backups
  • C. Ignored the ransom message and turned off the computer

Protection Tips

  • Keep your software and operating system up to date.
  • Use reliable antivirus and anti-malware tools.
  • Back up important files regularly to external or cloud storage.
  • Never open suspicious emails or download files from unknown sources.
