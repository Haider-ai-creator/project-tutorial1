About This Project

Purpose

The Cybersecurity Awareness Game is designed to educate users about essential cybersecurity concepts through interactive learning and engaging gameplay. It focuses on real-world threats such as phishing, ransomware, and social engineering to build safer digital habits.

Target Audience

  • Students: Introducing foundational cybersecurity knowledge early on.
  • Corporate Employees: Helping organizations reduce human error through training.
  • General Public: Empowering individuals to stay safe online.
  • Organizations: Offering scalable training solutions to meet compliance goals.
